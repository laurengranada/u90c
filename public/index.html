About

The Upper Ninety cup was inspired by the activism and compassion generated by the Austin community after hurricane Harvey. Thousands of Austinites rallied around those in need and gave of their time, money, and support, especially in the Austin soccer community.

The Upper Ninety Cup is a brand new fundraising tournament taking place during this summers world cup that isn't centered around big donors, expensive speakers, and high priced tickets. We want to have the Austin Community come to Upper Ninety and we want to come to y'all.


How do you participate?


Throughout the World Cup, English Premier League Supporter groups will be competing to raise money for Upper Ninety at their respective bars.

The groups and bars that are participating are as follows:

Austin Spurs at Mr. Tramps
Austin Gooners at the Tavern
Austin Red at B.D. Rileys at Mueller
Manchester United at Black Sheep Lodge

The group that raises the most money at the end of the World Cup will receive the Upper Ninety Cup trophy at our Haymaker World Cup Watch Party. Each of the bars and supporters groups have pledged to raise money through various means. You can donate directly to their CrowdRise pages, you can purchase a Upper Ninety Gold at participating bars and a percentage of the funds raised will go towards Upper Ninety.

Please see participating bars to see how you can raise money.

If you have any questions you can always reach us at www.upperninety.org
